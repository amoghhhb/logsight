Powerful Features, Total Control

Deep System Scan

Scan specific directories or the entire filesystem with Admin Mode to find all relevant logs.

Real-time Monitor

Watch log files as they're written. See new entries appear instantly with live syntax highlighting.

Secure Encryption

All analysis reports are encrypted locally with a secure key before being saved or sent.

Custom Patterns

Define your own threat patterns using a simple `.txt` file. The app detects and highlights your custom regex.

Time-Range Filter

Narrow your analysis to a specific timeframe. Only analyze log entries created between your chosen dates and times.
